About Me!

As a software engineer at Conscious Technologies, I collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver top-notch software products that meet the needs and expectations of our clients. I prioritize effective communication, problem-solving, and a commitment to producing clean, maintainable code. I also consistently update my skills to stay informed about the latest industry trends, frameworks, and tools.

I have a strong background in full stack development, having successfully completed an internship at Luminar Technolab, where I leveraged the power of the MEAN and MERN stacks to develop end-to-end solutions that were dynamic, responsive, and scalable. I have a deep understanding of JavaScript, both on the front-end and back-end, allowing me to create user interfaces using React.js and Angular.js, and API endpoints and server-side functionalities using MongoDB, Node.js, and Express.js. I also have experience in software deployment and Astro.js. During my internship, I collaborated with cross-functional teams to gather project requirements and translated them into technical specifications. I played a pivotal role in the development and maintenance of web applications using React and Angular, enhancing user experiences and streamlining data access. My commitment to quality is evidenced by my participation in code reviews, debugging, and testing, contributing to the overall improvement of software quality.

My education, including a Bachelor of Commerce in Computer Programming and a Diploma in Logistics and Supply Chain Management, has equipped me with a diverse skill set. Additionally, I have complemented my formal education with certifications in relevant technologies and programming languages.

I am proficient in Angular, React.js, Vue JS, Astro JS, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, and have experience with GitHub, GitLab, Node.js, Postgres SQL, MySQL, MongoDB, and API development. I am enthusiastic about continuous learning and have demonstrated this by earning certifications in various areas, including AI engineering and web development.

With a strong foundation in both business and technology, I am passionate about technology and learning new skills, as evidenced by my multiple certifications in chat GPT and AI tools, HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS3, and my recognition as a learning enthusiast by Open Weaver. I also enjoy travelling and photography, which inspire me to explore new perspectives and challenge myself creatively. My goal is to apply my skills and knowledge to create innovative and impactful software solutions that can make a positive difference in the world.
